Learn DeFi Concepts

🤖 Automated Market Maker (AMM)

DEXs use mathematical formulas to determine token prices based on supply and demand, eliminating the need for traditional order books.

💧 Liquidity Pools

Users provide token pairs to pools, earning fees from trades. The ratio of tokens determines the exchange rate.

📉 Slippage

The difference between expected and actual trade prices due to market movement during transaction execution.

🏦 Yield Farming

Earn rewards by providing liquidity to pools. Returns come from trading fees and additional token incentives.
